ANLT 222 Group 4 Project: Books to Scrape Analysis

Project by Bai Wang

Code for Crawling

Mainly used BeautifulSoup for crawling. Data was transformed for analysis and plotting.

Data Visualization

Histogram showing distribution of book prices.

Bar chart of categories.

Boxplot of ratings across price ranges.

Swarm Plot of price distribution among different categories.

Summary and Findings

Most books are priced at 20~30 pounds or 40~50 pounds, but they often fail to meet reader expectations.

Recommendations

Books priced at 30~40 pounds tend to have higher ratings, indicating a more satisfying reading experience.
